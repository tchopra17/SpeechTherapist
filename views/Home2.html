What are we trying to solve?

  Our Solution

Our Solution

Learn

Lessons walk the users through a few word buckets that the users may struggle with. The application then analyzes users’ enunciation, provides the words based on their ability, and gives advice for improvement.

Challenge

"The gaming modules add value and entertaiment to the learning process. Users' voice is the controller. The only way to successfully complete the games is to pronouce each word correctly. If a words is pronounced incorrectly, the screen will provide feedback and an option of going back to the suggested learnring module.

Practice

Scientists have suggested that consistent practice over time can enhance specific actions more than others. With the tool, the users are given guidance and ample practice.

Support

The applicatoin provides parents and users the latest information on the current developments in the world of speech therapy. Parents can also exchange ideas and experiences on the forum.

How does it works?

Talk To Mario is an application that is designed to help children with articulation disorder. The application comprises of two streams that will help children practice their annunciation.
